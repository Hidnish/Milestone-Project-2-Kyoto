Welcome to Kyoto Day'n'Night

This is the first step through a journey you will treasure forever. Kyoto is one of the most ancient and beautiful cities in Japan, plenty of history, culture and art but also a place of leisure and fun. We made a selection of locations to visit that you cannot miss thoughout your journey to fully experience the greatness of this city.

Activities have been divided between DAY and NIGHT.
DAY activities include visiting historical sites, parks and gardens. NIGHT activities focus on treating yourself with the best restaurants, bars and clubs.

Historical Sites

Kyoto is home to some of the most antique historical landmakrs in Japan, from the To-ji Buddhist temple to Fushimi Inari Taisha, the popular shrine (displayed in the picture) dedicated to the Shinto god of rice.
Click here to see all the historical sites we have selected for you.

Gardens & Parks

This city is famous around the world not only for the preserved cultural relics, but also because of its beautiful nature. You will be astonished by the cherry blossoms and Shakkei, the art of "borrowing scenery" from the natural landscape into the view of a cultivated garden.
Click here to see all the gardens and parks we have selected for you.

Restaurants

When it comes to good food, Kyoto is the right place for you to visit. The city can offer you an unforgettable culinary experience if you know where to go, from delicious Soba noodles to the tipical Obanzai.
Click here to see all the restaurants we have selected for you.

Bars & Clubs

Last but not least comes the late night fun. Kyoto can provide great options for everyone, from the most sophisticated sake tasters to the party animals.
